## Research Overview

My work explores plasma interactions with materials, nanoparticles, and surfaces at atmospheric pressure, with emphasis on functional materials, sensing, and advanced manufacturing.

## Major Areas

### 1. Atmospheric-Pressure Plasma Processing
Cold plasma methods for material synthesis, deposition, annealing, and surface treatment.

### 2. Plasma–Nanoparticle Interactions
Plasma-driven changes in particle charging, transport, aggregation, morphology, and chemistry.

### 3. Plasma-enabled Sensors
Development of flexible and high-performance sensing materials using plasma-assisted processing.

### 4. Plasma and Additive Manufacturing
Use of plasma tools for microfabrication, printing, and materials integration.
