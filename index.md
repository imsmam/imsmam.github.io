## About

My research focuses on low-temperature and atmospheric-pressure plasma interactions with materials, nanoparticles, and surfaces, with applications in sensing, nanomaterial processing, and plasma-assisted manufacturing.

## Research Themes

### Atmospheric-Pressure Plasma Processing
Development of plasma-based methods for material synthesis, annealing, and surface modification.

### Plasma–Nanoparticle Interactions
Understanding plasma effects on nanoparticle charging, morphology, assembly, and functionalization.

### Plasma-enabled Sensors
Use of plasma-processed nanostructures in electrochemical and optical sensing platforms.

### Plasma-assisted Manufacturing
Integration of plasma science with additive manufacturing and device fabrication.
